SINGLE FLOW ROTARY JOINTS

Single Flow Rotary Joints  – We are one of the Leading Rotary Joints manufacturers, suppliers and Exporter, manufacturing Finest Single flow Rotary Joints in India to meet General Purpose application need of fluid transfer.

Mono Flow Rotating Joints are used, when supply and return lines are connected to opposite sides of the cylinder. The transfer of the medium in the Single-Passage or Single Flow Rotary Joints is in one direction through the joint. An outstanding feature is that media can be moved at high speeds with high pressures. Single-Passage Rotary Joints can be used for various applications within all types of industries for various Media Like Water ,Air/Hydraulic, Steam, coolant, etc

Special Design Multi Passages Rotating Joints allow use of Multi media in different Passages For example, water in passages 1 and 2 and hydraulic oil in passages 3 and 4 . This is Possible through vent holes between passages.
