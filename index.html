UNICORN ENGINEERS IN INDIA

“Micro Seals“ AN ISO 9001-2015 UKAS Certified Company Unicorn Engineers is a leading manufacturer of Mechanical Seal for Pump, Compressor, Agitator, Mixer, Rotary Union, Submersible Motor, Pneutch Filter, Colloid Mills, Blowers, Microniser etc. Mechanical Seal is used in Rotary Equipments to prevent Leakage of fluids through the rotating shaft. Mechanical seal surfaces are mirror polished where one rotates and other remain static. Therefore there is hardly any friction which helps in Energy Saving while in conventional Gland Packing system Energy is lost due to heat of friction.

Unicorn Engineers established in 1991 by young Technocrats to promote concept of 100% sealing of rotary shaft through Mechanical Seal. Unicorn Engineers has got reputation of solving critical problems of Mechanical Seals and providing best Quality goods at reasonable price with fast delivery. Our team is formed by Engineers in order to understand the critical problems of subject. We are committed to offer best designs, quality products and services to our customers at their satisfaction..

John Crane, Eagle Burgmann, Flowserve, Pillar, Sealol, Durametallic,Flexibox, Borgwarner, Chesterten, Roten etc.

Unicorn Engineers is catering almost all industries like Oil & Gas, Chemical, Petrochemicals, Power, Food & Beverages, Textile, Pharmaceuticals, Metal & Minerals, Mining, Automobile, Fertilizers & Chemicals, Cement etc. We offer complete range of Mechanical Seal designs including Single and Double cartridge seals, Multi Spring seals, Single Coil Spring seals,Metal bellows seals, Conical Spring seals, Elastomer Bellow Seals, PTFE Bellow seals as well as custom engineered seals. All Mechanical Seals are available in a wide range of materials and sizes.
