Leading Rotary Unions Manufacturers

"Micro Seals" Brand Rotary Unions was Developed with the Object to fulfill the needs of the current market of Rotary Unions in india with special Attention on providing a quality product, at an affordable price. The experience acquired by Micro Seals Team as a Leading Mechanical Seals Manufacturers has led to the construction of a wide range of Rotary unions.

Micro Seals Rotary unions are used for the leak proof transfer of fluids (such as steam, water, air or oil) from stationary supply lines to rotating machine parts. Micro Seals Rotary unions allow the efficient transmission of cooling Water, Air, Oil, Hydraulic Fluids and Gases etc…. into Rotating Shafts, Rollers & rotating assemblies .

Micro seals is a Leading Rotary Unions Manufacturers which offer Complete Range of Rotary unions products to meet a wide variety of applications requiring various medium, sizes, pressures, speeds, and temperatures.Micro Seals Rotary unions are used in the machine tool, textile, plastic, printing,paper, rubber, food, steel, chemical, and energy industry. Our professional expertise allows us to select the right rotary Unions for each application.

As a Best Rotary Unions Manufacturers we offer wide range & Variety of standard Rotary Unions for General Industrial Application . We can modify our standard rotary unions to suit individual customers exact Application for operating performance, cost, and delivery.
